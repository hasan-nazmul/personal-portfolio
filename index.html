Origin Story: The Making of a Coder

Greetings, traveler! I am Nazmul Hasan, a Computer Science and Engineering student from the International Islamic University Chittagong. My story isn't about born heroes; it's about a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a deep-seated love for the digital universe. From a young age, I was fascinated by how a few lines of code could create entire worlds, solve complex puzzles, and build things never before imagined.

This journey has been a thrilling one—filled with epic battles in coding contests and collaborative quests with my fellow adventurers. My path has been guided by mentors and fueled by a passion for creating, whether it's building games, training AIs, or simply solving a tough problem. This portfolio is a chronicle of my adventures, my missions, and the superpowers I've gained along the way.
